About Me

AI & ML Engineer with published research, leadership experience, and expertise in building intelligent systems

I'm Harinivas M, an AI & ML Engineer at Charteredways Services Private Limited, specializing in building scalable software systems and integrating machine learning models into real-world applications. Currently pursuing B.Tech in AI & ML at Sri Shakthi Institute of Engineering and Technology, Coimbatore.

My expertise lies in Python, TensorFlow, and AWS Cloud services, with professional experience building data-driven solutions and intelligent automation systems. I've published three research papers in international journals on AI-based retail systems, crop recommendation using ETL, and landmark detection using computer vision.

Beyond technical work, I've served as Treasurer of The AI Avalon association, managing financial operations and event execution. Previously led the Media Team coordinating content creation and social media strategy. I'm passionate about solving complex problems through AI/ML research and creating systems that drive real-world impact.
